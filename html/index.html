Science fiction has roots reaching as far back as the second century (the short novel "A True Story", authored by the satirist Lucian).



Although it may be difficult to compare modern Science Fiction with its ancient beginnings, many of the same themes and tropes can be found such as:

  • travel to other worlds
  • extraterrestrial lifeforms
  • interplanetary warfare
  • artificial life

Modern science fiction can be broken down into many different sub-genres. Similar to music sub-genres, works of science fiction can often transcend the bounds of sub-genres.



Categorization of sub-genres can often times be subjective. It's not uncommon for Science Fiction novels, films, and shows to transcend multiple sub-genres.

Science fiction is by far my favorite form of entertainment due to the strong technological themes, the speculative nature of the gnere, and the phyilosophical questions that are often prominent. Hard Science Fiction is my person favorite sub-genre. This site is dedicated to exploring some of my personal favorite works of science fiction.
